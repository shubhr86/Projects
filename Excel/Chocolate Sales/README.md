# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Dashboard 

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents a dynamic **Chocolate Sales Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and calculated columns.

The dashboard analyzes chocolate product sales performance across multiple countries and regions for the year 2022. It provides business insights into revenue trends, regional performance, sales efficiency, and salesperson rankings.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total revenue and shipment performance
- Identify top-performing regions, countries, and products
- Evaluate sales tier contribution
- Measure revenue efficiency per box
- Rank top 10 sales persons dynamically
- Detect seasonal sales patterns

---

## 🗂 Dataset Structure

The dataset includes the following columns:

- Sales Person  
- Country  
- Region  
- Product  
- Date 
- Month (Derived)  
- Amount  
- Boxes Shipped  
- Revenue Per Box (Derived)  
- Sales Tier (Derived)

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers (Month, Country, Region, Year)
- Calculated Fields
- Conditional Sorting (Top 10 filter)
- Revenue per Box calculation
- Data cleaning & standardization
- Dynamic KPI Cards using GETPIVOTDATA
- Custom dashboard design & layout formatting

---

## 📌 Key KPIs

- **Total Revenue:** $6,183,625  
- **Total Boxes Shipped:** 177,007  
- **Average Revenue per Box:** $105.7  
- **Top Product:** Smooth Silky Salty  
- **Top Region:** Oceania  
- **Top Country:** Australia  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Revenue Performance
- Total revenue reached $6.18M in 2022.
- Medium tier contributed the largest share of revenue (43%).
- Smooth Silky Salty was the highest-performing product.

### 2️⃣ Regional Analysis
- Oceania generated the highest regional revenue.
- North America followed closely behind.
- Asia and Europe showed lower revenue per box compared to North America.

### 3️⃣ Country Contribution
- Australia was the top contributing country.
- USA and UK maintained strong revenue performance.
- Canada showed comparatively moderate contribution.

### 4️⃣ Salesperson Performance
- Top 10 sales persons contributed a significant share of revenue.
- Revenue distribution shows moderate concentration among high performers.

### 5️⃣ Efficiency Analysis
- North America achieved the highest revenue per box (~119.9).
- Europe and Asia showed lower average revenue per box (~90–92), indicating potential pricing strategy differences.

### 6️⃣ Seasonal Trends
- Revenue peaked in June.
- A mid-year spike indicates possible seasonal demand or campaign impact.
- Slight decline observed in late summer months.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing investments in Oceania and North America.
- Optimize pricing strategies in Asia and Europe.
- Analyze June sales drivers and replicate strategy.
- Introduce performance incentives for lower-performing sales staff.
- Monitor revenue per box to maintain pricing efficiency.
